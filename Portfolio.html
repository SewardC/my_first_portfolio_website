Our Mission Statement
  • Our mission is to serve as a platform to help aspiring young writers improve their work through constructive criticism.

Features
  • A daily post for school students with professional feedback to study for.
  • Feedback/Comment limit
  • Notification System.

Possible Consumer
  • Demographics: Susan is a junior in High school at age 17 and she loves participating in English Class.
  • Geographics: Susan lives in the busy streets of New York City.
  • Psychographics: Susan is interested in literature will do any writing activities or independently read during their spare time.
  • Buying Patterns: Susan is not a spender, but will spend money on some materials for literary purpose like pencils, paper, books, journal etc.
